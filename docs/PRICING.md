# Pillow AI - Pricing Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the pricing strategy for Pillow AI, targeting small and local businesses. Our service is built on top of Retell AI's voice agent platform.

---

## Cost Structure (Retell AI)

Understanding our underlying costs is essential for sustainable pricing.

| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| Voice Engine (ElevenLabs/OpenAI) | $0.07-0.08/min |
| LLM (GPT-4o) | ~$0.02/min |
| Knowledge Base | $0.005/min |
| Phone Number Rental | $2/month |
| **Total Estimated Cost** | **~$0.13-0.15/min** |

### Additional Retell Costs to Consider
- Batch dialing: $0.005 per number
- Branded caller ID: $0.10 per call
- Extra concurrent calls (beyond 20): $8/month per line
- Additional knowledge bases (beyond 10): $8/month each

## Pricing Psychology

### Price Point Perception (Small Business)

| Price Range | Perception |
|-------------|------------|
| Under $50 | "Let me try it" - Low commitment |
| $50-150 | "Reasonable if it works" - Value-conscious |
| $150-350 | "Needs to show clear ROI" - Serious consideration |
| $350+ | "Significant investment" - Requires approval |

### Key Insights
1. **$49 is the sweet spot** for initial adoption
2. **Annual plans** should offer 20% discount (2 months free)
3. **Setup fees** optional: $199 one-time OR waived with annual commitment
4. **Overage alerts** at 80% usage prevent bill shock

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Early Adopter Pricing (Month 1-3)
- 30% discount on all plans
- Effective pricing: $35, $99, $249
- Goal: Acquire first 100 customers
- Lock in pricing for 12 months

### Phase 2: Referral Program (Month 3+)
- $25 credit for successful referrals
- Referred customer gets first month 50% off
- Small businesses trust peer recommendations

### Phase 3: Industry Packages (Month 6+)
- Pre-built industry templates
- "Restaurant AI Receptionist" - $79/mo
- "Salon Booking Assistant" - $99/mo
- "Medical Office Agent" - $149/mo
- Faster sales cycle with turnkey solutions
